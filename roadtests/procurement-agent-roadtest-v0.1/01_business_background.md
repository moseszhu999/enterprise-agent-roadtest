# 01 — Business Background

## Company

A mid-sized manufacturing company produces optical communication equipment for enterprise customers.

The company is currently fulfilling a customer order that must be delivered in **14 days**.

## Item to purchase

```text
Optical Communication Control Board Module
```

This component is required for the final assembly stage.

## Business pressure

The production line consumes this component every day. If supply is delayed, the company may face:

- production stoppage,
- delayed customer delivery,
- penalty risk,
- emergency purchasing cost,
- management escalation.

## Key point

This is not a normal lowest-price purchasing task.

The Agent must balance:

- purchase price,
- lead time,
- inventory coverage,
- supplier delivery history,
- quality risk,
- payment terms,
- capacity limit,
- approval rules,
- stop-line risk.

A procurement Agent that only selects the cheapest supplier should fail this roadtest.
